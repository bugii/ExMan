Exporting your data

Note that any personal data that we have collected will be anoymized/hashed during this exporting process. This includes the following:

  • Notification content: Removing content and hashing the name of the sender
  • Active Windows: Hashing the window titles
  • Goals: Hashing the goals
Furthermore, after the export has completed, the file will be opened and you have the chance of removing information that you do not want to share with us.
